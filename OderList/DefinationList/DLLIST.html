Computer components

CPU-
The CPU (Central Processing Unit) acts as the brain, executing instructions and performing calculations
RAM-
RAM is a type of volatile memory that stores data and instructions that the CPU is actively using.
SSD-
SSDs provide non-volatile storage for data, meaning the data is retained even when the computer is turned off.


Social media platforms and their purposes

Facebook:
Primarily for connecting with friends and family, sharing updates, and joining groups based on shared interests.
Instagram:
A visual platform for sharing photos and videos, exploring trends, and engaging with influencers.
TikTok:
Known for short-form videos, trends, and creative content, particularly popular among younger audiences.
Snapchat:
A platform for sharing disappearing photos and videos, primarily used for casual communication with friends.
WhatsApp:
A popular messaging app for private and group communication, also featuring status updates and media sharing.
